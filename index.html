Scott Ogletree, PhD

I am a researcher who uses spatial thinking to understand and solve problems.

My broad research interest areas include:
Human-nature interaction, Outdoor recreation, Space and place


I defended my PhD dissertation in July 2019 at Clemson University. My dissertation research investigated how urban greenspaces are related to crime in cities.

Other things I'm interested in include playing outside, geographic data science, design, maps & cartography, statistics, data visualization, and bikes.

As of September 2019 I am a postdoctoral research scholar at North Carolina State University under the direction of Dr. Aaron Hipp & Dr. Myron Floyd
